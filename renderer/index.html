How it works

The art that decays itself.

Ephemera generates live art from four different sources. Each source decays when left still or unattended. Interaction restores it.

Camera Mode

Stand still and watch your silhouette dissolve. Wave your hands to sweep clarity back across decayed areas. Enable AI Shield to protect your body outline automatically.

MP3 Mode

Upload a song. You will see 4 distinct oscilloscope rows for each frequency band (Sub-bass to Air). If ignored, the wave lines decay into static and the audio distorts. Hover over a wave to clean it and restore the song.

Image Mode

Upload a photo. Over time, it will begin to fall apart based on the selected Style physics. Use your mouse as a brush to wipe away the decay.

The Concept

Stillness is not peace. It is permission for entropy.

Ephemera is generative art built on the principle that observation changes the observed. Your presence is the only force preventing the work from consuming itself.
